Project Title:
My Favorite Movie

Make a poster about your favorite movie.

Join a team to do the project!

You can make your poster individually but doing the project in a team would make the things easier and be much more fun!

  • Make a team of 4, 5 or 6 people.
  • Brainstorm the content of your project.
  • Share the tasks and responsibilities with your team members.
  • Collect the information and prepare the materials you will need for the project. You may need to make a research to find important details and pictures.
  • Don't forget: The project will be paper based. You will need the materials like scissors, glue, color pens etc.

You can start the project now!

The Movies

Elements of Movies

You should give some important details about your favorite movie. These are the Elements of Movies.

Movie Title

Don't forget the write the Title of the Movie on your poster. This is the most important element of movie. It should be big enough and placed in the center of the poster.

Movie Studio

The movie studio is the company which makes the movie. Pixar , Disney, and Marvel are some of the movie studios. Which movie studio made your favorite movie?

Movie Genre

What's the genre of your favorite movie? Is it an action movie? Fantasy? Comedy? Animation? Don't forget the give this information in your project.

Setting

Setting is the time or place of the story? Does your movie set in a city, castle, a magical land, or in a hounted house?

Stars

Movie stars are the actors played in the movies. Who are the movie stars played in your favorite movie?

Characters

Characters are the heroes or villains of your movie. It's different than the movie starts. For example, Spiderman is the character in the movie but Tom Holland in the movie star played the character.

Presentation

The most important part of the project is PRESENTATION! Your speaking skills will be graded during your presentation. To get higher grades, you should follow the followin points during your presentation:

  • Speak confidently.
  • Look at your audience during the presentation. Make eye contact!
  • Be careful about the pronunciation of the words.
  • Be sure that your sentences are grammatically correct.
  • Speak slowly and loudly enough.
